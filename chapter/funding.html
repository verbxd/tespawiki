Recommended Steps

Generalized. May vary per campus.

  1. Hold a strategy meeting with your officers well in advance of your event. Identify goals, constraints, responsibilities, and a basic agenda — what does your ideal event look like, and what does it accomplish?
    1. Naturally, a fundraising event should raise money, but it can also be social or competitive.
  2. After your strategy meeting, select a venue for your event.
    1. Reach out to local businesses — many have fundraising programs specifically for students.
    2. Consult with your faculty advisor or events office to see if your campus has fundraising rules.
  3. Publish your event on an events platform like Facebook or MyTespa, then use social media and available campus marketing options to promote your event to members and students.
    1. Since you’re selling something, make sure you clearly advertise the price and value.
  4. Before the event, arrive early with your officers to set up the room, prepare the sales process, and review roles and responsibilities.
    1. Consider having a digital payment option available, like PayPal, Square, or Venmo.
  5. During your event, stay alert, be flexible, and prioritize the attendee experience. Nobody expects a club event to run perfectly, but members do expect events to feel like a welcoming place. If you stay positive and provide a great attendee experience, you can’t have a bad event.

Considerations

Generalized. May vary per campus.

  • Some fundraising event suggestions…
    • Food and drink sales, “Play the Pro” competitions, charity LAN events, merchandise sales, paid raffles, coaching or tutorial sessions.
  • Sell the “why”, not the ‘what” — members are going to care more about why they’re contributing to the cause, not what they’ll be paying for.
  • Look into whether your campus offers tax-exempt club bank accounts. If students are paying to your personal bank account, you’ll have to pay taxes on any funds you raise.
    • Some club bank accounts will have restrictions on when and how you can withdraw money.
